Playing a concert can be great fun! Or it can be totally scary! Sometimes both at the same time.

So we've put together a list of our top tips for great concerts.

Write out a set-list

A set-list is a list of all your songs in the order that you'll play them.

Make copies for everyone.

Print it BIG, in case its down by your feet or if the lighting isn't that good.

Remember your spares.

Some instruments need to have parts that need to be replaced in case they wear out or break. For example:

  • Guitar strings
  • Saxaphone or Clarinet reeds
  • Drumsticks

Make a list of the parts you'll need and keep plenty packed along for the event.

Plan where you'll be on stage

Before you set up on stage, be sure you know where you'll be on stage.

  • Will the drums be in the center or off to the side?
  • Is the power outlet close enough to the amplifiers?

Know where you're going, know when you'll be on.
